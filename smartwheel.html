Smart Wheelchair

As a part of my internship at Bost Robotics Ltd. (Guangzhou, China), I designed a smart electric wheelchair with obstacle detection using arduino. To take this project further, we designed several modular control blocks, such as jaw control and assistant control that can be easily installed on the wheelchair. I applied for utility patent for the idea of modular electric wheelchair in China (Patent number CN201520208133.8).
